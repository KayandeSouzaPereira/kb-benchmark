# In-app notification retention

In-app (bell icon) notifications are kept for 60 days then auto-archived.
E-mail notification logs follow the webhook delivery log retention policy
instead (see
[../webhooks/delivery-log-retention.md](../webhooks/delivery-log-retention.md)).
